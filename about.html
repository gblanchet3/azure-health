About

Healing beyond the physical.

Amanda Blanchet, PA-C · Reiki Master

I'm a licensed Physician Assistant with over eight years of experience in emergency medicine, where I've worked in high-acuity settings caring for patients across a wide range of conditions.

Through that work, I developed a deep respect for the power and importance of Western medicine — but I also began to see its limitations.

Much of what I encountered focused on stabilization, symptom management, and crisis intervention. While essential, it often left little space for deeper restoration, or for addressing the full complexity of a person's experience. Over time, it became clear that many people improve — but still don't feel fully well, fully themselves, nor in a state of truly thriving.

That realization led me to expand my training beyond the traditional medical model.

I have always been guided by intuition, and over time began to deepen that awareness through formal training. I became a Reiki Master and began studying energy-based healing as a way to better support the connection between mind, body, and nervous system regulation.

I believe the mind-body connection is incredibly powerful. We can, often unintentionally, guide ourselves into states of illness — but we also have the capacity to guide ourselves back toward healing. "Dis-ease" can arise when the body is not given the internal and external conditions it needs to regulate, restore, and return to balance.

My approach is grounded in both clinical knowledge and intuitive awareness. I focus on creating the conditions where healing can happen in a supported, natural way — where the body can shift out of stress, the mind can quiet, and a deeper level of restoration becomes possible.

Each session is designed to feel calm, supportive, and intentional, meeting you exactly where you are.
