Service

Integrative Reset Session

A guided, in-home experience designed to support deep nervous system regulation, emotional reset, and whole-body restoration. Amanda comes to you.

Who this is for

Meeting the body where it is.

This session is designed for individuals who:

  • Are experiencing stress, anxiety, or burnout
  • Are seeking a different approach after not finding lasting relief through conventional methods
  • Want to move beyond symptom management into deeper healing
  • Are open to a more integrative, whole-person approach
What this supports

An intentional shift toward regulation.

  • Nervous system regulation
  • Emotional processing
  • Release of chronic stress patterns
  • Increased clarity and connection to self
  • A deeper sense of internal balance
What to expect

The arc of a session.

  1. Pre-Session Preparation

    A virtual consultation one to two weeks prior to review your medical history, discuss goals, and ensure safety and readiness.

  2. Arrival & Grounding

    We begin with a short conversation and time to settle in, followed by guided grounding and breathwork.

  3. Energy Work

    You'll rest comfortably while receiving hands-on, intuitive energy work designed to support regulation and restoration.

  4. Ketamine-Assisted Component (when appropriate)

    For appropriate clients, ketamine may be incorporated in a medically guided format to support increased neuroplasticity and deeper access to internal experience.

    Read about ketamine safety →

  5. Closing & Integration

    Time to gently return, reflect, and transition out of the session.
